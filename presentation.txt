1. Segmentation ModuleStatement
	 1: Our system utilizes intelligent segmentation to distinguish between the Region of Interest (ROI) containing critical diagnostic data and the Region of Non-Interest (RONI).Statement
	 2: This differentiation allows us to apply maximum protection to sensitive medical areas while ensuring that the visual integrity required for an accurate doctor’s diagnosis is never compromised.




2. Hash Generation ModuleStatement
	 1: We generate a unique, high-security cryptographic hash of the ROI to serve as an immutable "digital fingerprint" of the patient’s diagnostic data.Statement 	2: Because this hash is hypersensitive to change, even a single-pixel alteration in the medical scan will result in a total hash mismatch, ensuring zero-tolerance for unauthorized data manipulation.




3.Embedding ModuleStatement 1: The Embedding Module securely integrates the authentication hash directly into the image's bitstream, creating a self-contained, secure medical file.Statement 2: By strategically hiding this data within the image itself, we facilitate safe medical image transmission across platforms without the need for external metadata files or vulnerable third-party databases.
